I never knew why people built blogs, or even spent a second of their days writing stuff publicly (or even privately), but recently I read a tweet from someone talking about how Turbopuffer creator Simon Eskildsen had a blog with posts dated back to 2010. I wish that I had written about my opinions and what I went through, I wish I had something that I could look and see the opinions of the Vinicius from 5, or 10 years ago. Unfortunately, memory is a faulty data store, and you will definitely forget the small things, and sometimes even the big things. That's why we write things down, we record things, and I wish I did. But the best moment to start is always now, so here it goes.

I don't expect people to read my ramblings on software and whatever else, but I do expect the Vinicius from 5 or 10 years from now to read his old ramblings, so this is for him.

That's it for now. More soon.
